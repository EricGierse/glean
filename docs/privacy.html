Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

Glean ("Glean", "we", "us") is a Chrome extension that helps you collect and organize receipts and expenses. Glean is built local-first: by default, your data lives on your own device, not on our servers. This policy explains what we handle and why.

1. Data stored on your device

Your captured receipts, categories, settings and preferences are stored locally in your browser (via the extension's storage). This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us unless you explicitly enable a feature that requires it (see below). Uninstalling the extension or using "Delete all" removes this data.

2. Accounts & sign-in

Creating an account lets you unlock Pro and sync settings. You can sign in with Google, Apple, or email. When you do, we process the minimum needed to identify your account:

  • Your email address and, where provided, your name and avatar.
  • A unique account identifier and authentication token.

We do not receive or store your Google/Apple password. Sign-in is handled by those providers under their own privacy policies.

3. Inbox connections (Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail)

If you choose to connect an inbox, Glean requests read-only access for the sole purpose of finding receipts, invoices and order confirmations. Specifically:

  • We scan messages to detect receipts and extract details (merchant, amount, date, tax).
  • We do not read, store, or use unrelated email content, and we never send marketing.
  • Connections are optional and revocable at any time, from Glean's settings or from your Google/Microsoft/Apple account security page.

Glean's use of information from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

4. AI receipt scanning

When you use the AI photo-scan feature, the image you upload is sent to our AI processing provider to extract the amount and a likely category. Images are processed only to return that result, are not used to train models, and are not retained beyond what's needed to complete the request. If you prefer, on-device text recognition can be used instead.

5. Payments

Paid subscriptions are processed by our payment provider (e.g., Stripe). We do not store your full card details. We retain only what's required to manage your subscription (such as plan status and billing region).

6. What we don't do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not show ads or use third-party advertising trackers.
  • We do not access more of your inbox or browsing than needed to find receipts.

7. Your choices & rights

  • Export or delete your local data anytime from Glean's settings.
  • Disconnect any inbox or sign out at any time.
  • Request access to or deletion of any account data we hold by contacting us.

8. Changes

We may update this policy as Glean evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with a new "Last updated" date.

9. Contact

Questions about your privacy, or a request to access or delete your data? Open an issue on our GitHub issues page.
